About Plant Mission Notebook

Plant Mission Notebook is a study web and game where learners scan real plants using a Teachable Machine model. Correct scans unlock mission progress and create collectible Plant Notebook cards with photo evidence, plant type, and Hong Kong status notes.

Core Idea

Instead of only searching online for plant names, users play through missions. Every scan is a challenge with confidence thresholds, rewards, and clear progression.

Learning Flow

After each correct scan, the unlocked card stores the plant photo, plant type, mission note, and Hong Kong status information. Students and public users can review these cards to know what kind of plant they found.

Audience

Designed for school projects, biology clubs, and beginner botany learners who need both motivation (game) and knowledge structure (academy guidance).

Technology

Built as a front-end web app with TensorFlow.js and Teachable Machine image model inference in browser. Plant Book data is saved locally in the browser for quick access.
