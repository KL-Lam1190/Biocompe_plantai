Academy Guide

This page helps students understand what each unlocked plant card means. In the Scanner Game, every correct scan stores a photo and also adds learning details like plant type and academy study direction.

Flowering Shrub

Focus on petals, stem texture, and pollinator behavior. Compare flower structure from different angles.

Examples: rose, hibiscus

Succulent

Focus on water storage tissues and spine layout. Observe drought adaptation in shape and color.

Examples: cactus, aloe

Spore Plant

Focus on frond segmentation and sori pattern. Learn how spore reproduction differs from flowering plants.

Examples: fern

Bryophyte

Focus on moisture dependence and micro-habitat. Track how the colony expands after rain.

Examples: moss

Tree

Focus on bark texture, branch architecture, and canopy shape. Compare young and mature growth forms.

Examples: oak, pine

Grass Family

Focus on node spacing and blade veins. Observe response to sunlight and soil moisture changes.

Examples: grass, bamboo
